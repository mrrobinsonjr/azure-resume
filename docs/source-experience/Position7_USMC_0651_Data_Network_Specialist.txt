0651 – Data Network Specialist
United States Marine Corps, 3rd Marine Division
Kaneohe Bay, HI
2000–2004

Role Summary:
Supported base-wide classified and unclassified networks for 3,000+ users. Provided network administration, troubleshooting, system maintenance, and cybersecurity enforcement in mission-critical environments.

Responsibilities:
- Delivered on-site, remote, and phone support for Windows and UNIX users.
- Maintained SIPR/NIPR network services and communications.
- Supported routing/switching equipment and network infrastructure.
- Administered user accounts, permissions, and workstation configurations.
- Applied DoD security requirements and supported INFOSEC/OPSEC.
- Provided communications support during field exercises and deployments.

Achievements:
- Improved operational readiness via stable SIPR/NIPR communications.
- Reduced service interruptions with effective troubleshooting.
- Strengthened cybersecurity across unit systems and networks.

Technologies:
Cisco networking, Windows, UNIX, SIPR/NIPR, OPSEC/INFOSEC, tactical communications.
