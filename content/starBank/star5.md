# STAR 5 — Leading Through a Matrixed Organization
(Leadership Without Authority)
Situation
Cloud and security efforts spanned multiple teams, organizations, and stakeholders with no single line of authority. – JRSS Security Applications Architect.
Task
Drive architectural consistency and security outcomes across a matrixed environment.
Action
• Facilitated a data driven decision making process, by creating a POC to alleviate stakeholder concerns about latency.
Served as a technical authority and advisor rather than directive manager
• Produced architecture documentation, trade studies, and recommendations
• Briefed senior leadership to gain alignment and buy-in
• Built trust by balancing mission urgency with risk awareness
Result
• POC Proved latency concerned were unfounded and which allowed the decision to move forward ahead of schedule
• Achieved alignment across teams without formal authority
• Accelerated decision-making through clear architectural guidance
• The architectural guidance I established, reduced configuration drift by 20%

“How do you influence without authority?”
“Tell me about a leadership challenge”
