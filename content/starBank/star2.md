# STAR 2 — Automating STIG Compliance at Scale
(Security Architecture, Automation, Compliance)
Situation
STIG compliance checks and reporting were largely manual, time-consuming, and error-prone across Cloud One environments.
Task
Design an automated approach to continuously assess and enforce STIG compliance while improving audit readiness.
Action
• Architected automated STIG assessments using Evaluate-STIG
• Integrated results directly into STIG Manager for centralized visibility
• Implemented automated remediation and enforcement using DSC, GPO, Ansible, and Packer
• Ensured solutions aligned with RMF and continuous monitoring requirements
Result
• Enabled continuous STIG compliance instead of point-in-time scans
• Reduced manual audit effort and improved reporting accuracy – Automated compliance across a globally distributed cloud, Reducing audit prep time by 40% per cycle.
• Increased confidence during inspections and ATO sustainment activities
