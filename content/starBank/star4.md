# STAR 4 — Enabling DevSecOps Without Slowing Teams
(DevSecOps, Influence, Architecture Judgment)
Situation
Development teams wanted faster deployment cycles, while security requirements risked becoming blockers.
Task
Enable DevSecOps in a way that preserves security while improving delivery speed.
Action
• Partnered with dev teams to redefine their approval workflows, moving them from manual gatekeeping to policy driven guardrails
• Architected CI/CD pipelines with security controls baked in (not bolted on)
• Used Terraform and policy-driven guardrails instead of manual approvals improving the process of deploying resources.
• Partnered with development teams to align security with delivery goals
• Used Merge Request Pipelines & checks to ensure code works/secure before it enters the target branch
• Focused on automation, inheritance, and standardization
Result
• Enforced least privileged access with for automated service principles
• Faster, repeatable deployments without sacrificing compliance
• 20% estimated reduction in lead time for change
• Reduced friction between security and engineering teams
• Improved overall platform maturity and trust
