# STAR 6 — Log Ingestion & Security Visibility
(Operations, Monitoring, Architecture Thinking)
Situation
Operating with fragmented visibility across common service and mission application environments, meaning an intrusion or performance bottleneck could go undetected for critical periods of time, threatening mission resilience.
Task
Onboarded Mission Apps data from customer subscriptions to common shared services for monitoring, analytics, security, compliance, and visibility
Action
• Designed log ingestion pipelines from cloud platforms into centralized analytics tools (Elastic/SIEM)
• Ensured alignment with RMF continuous monitoring requirements
• Collaborated with operations and security teams on use cases
Result
• Prior to the this ingestion pipeline, an intrusion could go undetected for days,
• Post implementation we achieved near real time visibility reducing the mean time to detect MTD from days to minutes
• Improved situational awareness and incident response
• Supported audit and compliance reporting
• Enabled proactive monitoring across environments
