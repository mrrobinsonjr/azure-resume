# STAR 3 — Supporting Cloud One RMF & ATO Sustainment
(Governance, Risk Management, Stakeholder Trust)
Situation
Cloud One operates large-scale, multi-cloud DoD environments requiring ongoing RMF compliance and ATO sustainment across many systems.
Task
Serve as senior cybersecurity and architecture lead supporting RMF execution and continuous authorization, for the Common Computer Environment (shared services) and customer Mission Applications.
Action
• Developed and maintained SAP, SAR, and POA&M artifacts
• Documented inheritable cloud controls to reduce system-owner burden
• Worked directly with assessment teams and system owners to resolve findings
• Embedded security controls into CI/CD pipelines to shift compliance left
Result
• Improved audit readiness and reduced ATO friction – Reduced audit prep by 40% saving 150 man hours per year.
• 150 man hours * $150 an hour = $22500 per system, scaled across common services. Freeing up time allowing for mission critical actions
• Streamlined authorization processes across Cloud One platforms
• Facilitated customer onboarding and support for new mission partners uses Teams and Slack to manage expectations and transparency.
Use for:
“How do you handle RMF in cloud environments?”
“How do you balance compliance and agility?”
